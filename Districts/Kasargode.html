Saptha Bhasha Sangama Bhoomi

KASARAGOD WEATHER
Lying at the northern tip of Kerala bounded by the Western ghats in the east and Arabian sea in the west; twelve rivers flowing across its terrain, Kasaragod is an enchanting beauty of Nature’s creations.The landscape is dominated by the characteristic coconut palms accompanying rolling hills and streams flowing into the sea. The landscape is dotted with tiled-roof buildings, topped with the famous Kasaragod tiles made with the local hard red clay and typically walled with laterite blocks. Older houses are commonly found with elaborate woodwork.
There are different views on the derivation of the name “KASARAGOD”. One view is that it is the combination of two Sanskrit words kaasaara (which means lake or pond) and kroda (which means a place where treasure is kept). Another view is that it is the place where Kaasaraka trees (Strychnos nux vomica or Kaanjiram or Kaaraskara) are in abundance. Both views are relavant as there are large number of rivers, lakes and ponds in the coastal belt of the district besides thick flora consisting of innumerable varieties of trees, shribs etc. particularly plentitude of Kaasaraka trees.


Top Sights


Bekal Fort

Built in the 17th century, the Bekal Fort in Kasaragod, Kerala is the largest fort in the State. Fashioned like a huge keyhole, this historic marvel bordered by a gorgeous beach offers spectacular view of the Arabian Sea.

Ranipuram

Ranipuram, about 85 kms from Kasaragod town and about 43 kms away from Kanhangad, stands at a height of 750 m above sea level and is the region of highest altitude in the district. The region was formerly known as Madathumala. The extensive forests here merge with the Coorg mountains of Karnataka.

Theanvarikallu Waterfalls

Theanvarikallu is the biggest Water falls in North Kerala, located 45 km from kanhangad, 4 km from malam, also known as thanmalayar waterfalls

Kappil Beach

Large spaces of pristine sand and a divine water body in front with no other disturbance whatsoever is something people seek in growing numbers these days. Among the finest and cleanest beaches in God’s Own Country, this is a destination perfect for those who seek solitude and a place to face their own selves in peace.
